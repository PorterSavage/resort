Travel and Transit Options

Directions to Resort

  • By Ferry:
    Take the Harrison Port ferry, available on Wednesdays and Fridays every 2 hours from 2pm to 4pm.
  • By Plane:
    Charter planes are available for booking at Harrison airport, by appointment only. Not available from October to March.
